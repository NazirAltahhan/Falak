Welcome to your Falak Educational Center

One of the most severe afflictions that has afflicted Arab-Islamic countries is the displacement of Muslim people around the world,
especially European countries and Turkey. What this displacement has led to in these countries is the loss of the Arabic language, learning the basics of religion that every Muslim must know, and the loss of the Friday sermon in Arabic.
Those who are most affected by this are young people and young children.

But by the grace of God Almighty and His blessings upon us, reformers who understood this great problem were made available to us in Turkey, especially in Istanbul (Constantinople in ancient times),
so they began as quickly as possible to open Arab-Islamic centres, concerned with:
Friday sermon in Arabic, teaching the Arabic language and the basics of the true Islamic religion, for all ages.

This Falak Educational Center is one of the best centers in Istanbul, and this is an overview of it and the activities within it.
We ask God to reward them with all goodness on our behalf.
